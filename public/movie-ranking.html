The Best Movies According to Suji

My top 3 movies of all time.

Fault in our Stars

Sanam Teri Kasam

It is a beautiful love story in which perfect desi girl falling in love with a rugged boy against her family.

Barfi

Again this one is a perfect love story between physically and mentally challenged persons.
